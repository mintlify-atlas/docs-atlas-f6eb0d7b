NuvioTV is an open-source Android TV media player built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. It connects to the Stremio addon ecosystem — install any compatible addon to browse catalogs, resolve streams, and play media directly on your TV.

## What NuvioTV Does

NuvioTV acts as a **client-side playback interface**. It does not host or distribute any media. Instead, it delegates all content discovery and stream resolution to user-installed addons — each addon exposes a catalog of titles and a list of stream URLs when you select something to watch.

## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download NuvioTV">
    Grab the latest APK from [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/NuvioMedia/NuvioTV/releases/latest) and sideload it on your Android TV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in or continue as guest">
    Scan the QR code with your phone to sign in with a Nuvio account, or skip for a local-only setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install an addon">
    Open **Add-ons** from the sidebar, enter an addon URL or scan the QR code to manage addons from your phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start watching">
    Browse the Home screen catalogs, search for a title, open its detail page, and select a stream to begin playback.
  </Step>
</Steps>

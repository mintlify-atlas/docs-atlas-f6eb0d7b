This guide takes you from a freshly installed NuvioTV to playing your first video. The entire flow takes about five minutes. If you haven't installed the app yet, start with the [Installation](/installation) guide and come back here once the app is open.

<Note>
  NuvioTV shows no content on the Home screen until at least one addon is installed. Step 3 below is the most important step — skip it and the app will appear empty.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch NuvioTV and sign in (or skip)">
    The very first time you open NuvioTV you will see an **account sign-in screen** with a QR code. Scanning the code opens the NuvioTV sign-in page (`https://nuvio.tv/tv-login`) on your phone, where you can create or log in to an account.

    Signing in enables:
    - Multi-profile support with PIN protection
    - Settings and watch history sync across devices via Supabase
    - Automatic profile pulling on first launch

    If you want to start immediately without an account, tap **Skip** (or the equivalent dismiss action). You can sign in later from **Settings → Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your experience mode">
    After the sign-in step, NuvioTV asks you to choose an **experience mode**. This controls which settings surfaces and features are visible throughout the app.

    <Tip>
      If you are new to NuvioTV or the Stremio addon ecosystem, choose **Essential**. It keeps the interface focused — add-ons, playback basics, Trakt, and account settings — without exposing every tuning option at once. You can switch to Advanced at any time from **Settings → Experience mode**.
    </Tip>

    | Mode | What's included |
    |---|---|
    | **Essential** | Focused setup, add-ons, playback basics, Trakt, and account settings |
    | **Advanced** | Full settings, layout controls, catalog order, collections, plugins, and diagnostics |

    Select a card and NuvioTV saves your choice immediately and takes you to the main app (or to the addon setup screen if you chose Essential and have no addons yet).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Install your first addon">
    Navigate to **Add-ons** in the left sidebar (the puzzle-piece icon). The Add-on Manager screen offers two ways to install addons:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Paste a manifest URL">
        Every Stremio-compatible addon publishes a `manifest.json` file at a well-known URL. To install one:

        1. Focus the URL input field at the top of the Add-on Manager screen.
        2. Enter the full manifest URL, for example:

           ```
           https://example.com/manifest.json
           ```

        3. Press the **Install** button (or confirm with the D-pad center key).

        NuvioTV fetches the manifest, validates it, and adds the addon to your list. Its catalogs will appear on the Home screen immediately.

        Addon manifest URLs always end with `/manifest.json`. If an addon's website gives you a `stremio://` link, replace `stremio://` with `https://` and the path should resolve to the manifest.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="QR code (phone browser)">
        NuvioTV runs a local HTTP server on your TV that serves a web management UI. To use it:

        1. Tap the **Manage from phone** button (phone icon) in the Add-on Manager.
        2. A QR code appears on screen. Scan it with your phone's camera.
        3. Your phone's browser opens the NuvioTV web UI, which shows your currently installed addons and lets you add new ones by pasting manifest URLs or browsing a catalog.
        4. Changes made in the phone browser are reflected on the TV in real time.

        This flow is especially convenient for typing long manifest URLs — your phone keyboard is far easier to use than a TV remote.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse or search for a title">
    After installing an addon, return to the **Home** screen (house icon in the sidebar). The addon's catalogs appear as horizontally scrollable rows, grouped by type (movies, series, etc.).

    To find something specific, navigate to **Search** (magnifying glass icon in the sidebar). Type a title using the on-screen keyboard — results are federated across all installed addons that support search.

    Navigating down from the sidebar automatically focuses the first content row on the active screen, so you can start browsing immediately with the D-pad.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a stream and play">
    Select any title from the Home or Search screen to open its **Detail page**. Here you will see metadata (synopsis, cast, year, rating) and — for series — a season/episode picker.

    From the Detail page you have two options:

    **Auto-play** — Press the primary **Play** button. NuvioTV queries all installed addons for available streams, applies your debrid resolver (if configured), and automatically starts the highest-quality match.

    **Manual stream selection** — Press **Choose stream** (or navigate to the stream list panel). Each stream entry shows its source addon name, resolution, and quality badges. Highlight a stream and confirm to start playback.

    Once playback begins, the player overlays standard TV controls: play/pause, seek bar, audio track switcher, subtitle track switcher, and a stream sources panel for switching mid-playback.

    <Note>
      For significantly better stream quality and reliability, configure a debrid service after your first playback. Go to **Settings → Debrid**, choose **Torbox** or **Premiumize**, and complete the device-code authentication flow. Once configured, NuvioTV resolves torrent-backed addon streams directly through your debrid account.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's next

Now that you have NuvioTV running with your first addon, explore these areas:

- **Add more addons** — The more addons you install, the broader your content catalog. You can reorder catalog rows in Advanced mode under **Settings → Catalog order**.
- **Configure debrid** — Go to **Settings → Debrid** to link Torbox, Premiumize, or Real-Debrid for fast, cached stream resolution.
- **Connect Trakt** — Go to **Settings → Trakt** to enable automatic watch history scrobbling.
- **Manage profiles** — Go to **Settings → Account → Manage profiles** to create additional profiles with independent watch histories and settings.
